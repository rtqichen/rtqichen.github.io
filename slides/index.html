Ricky Tian Qi Chen

I'm a Research Scientist at Meta Fundamental AI Research (FAIR) team in New York.

My research is on building simplified abstractions of the world through the lens of dynamical systems and flows.

I generally work on integrating structured transformations into probabilistic modeling, with the goal of improved interpretability, tractable optimization, or extending into novel areas of application.

Recently I've been focusing my research on building simulation-free methods for learning dynamical systems that model data generation processes.
